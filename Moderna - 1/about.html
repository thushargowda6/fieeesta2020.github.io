About us

Institute for Electrical and Electronics Engineers is an entity aimed at inculcating the sprirt of wisdom and knowledge. VVIET IEEE Student Branch was founded in 2009 under the guidance of our branch counselor, Dr. Bindu A Thomas. With events and workshops being conducted throughout the year, we make sure our members are kept up to date with the evolving needs of our technological world. In the Initial years, the student branch mainly focused on workshops and technical talks. Over the course of 10 years the student branch has grown immensely to what it is today. Our current chair is Mrs. Thushar D . With such achievements we have established ourselves as THE club to be in VVIET and strive to grow every year and have been doing so consistently over the years.
