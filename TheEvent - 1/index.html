About The Event

FIEEESTA'20 is an annual technical fest organised by VVIET-IEEE Student Branch, Mysuru. The objective of conducting this fest is to increase the technical knowledge among the students. Activities involved in this event are paper presentation and poster presentation.

Rules for the competition

  • Follow IEEE Format.
  • Topics can be Conceptual or model
  • Team can consist of minimum 2 & maximum of 4 members.
  • Abstract should not exceed 1 page and Paper should not exceed 15 pages.
  • Bring your college I-Card & receipt on event day.
  • Please bring your PowerPoint presentation on a pen-drive.
  • The teams will get 10 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
  • Please Mail your abstract, paper and ppt to vvietfieeesta@gmail.com . Difficulties will be addressed through the same email-id.
  • The mail with submissions should contain: Title- theme of the paper. Name of both the authors . Phone no of both the authors. E- mail ID .



Catagory Entry Fee per team member
IEEE Member Rs75/-
Non IEEE Member Rs100/-

*Note : Food will be provided.
